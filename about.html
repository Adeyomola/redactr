About Redactr
Redactr is basically a tool for scrambling words in a body of text. On the homepage, when you click on the Redact Your Text button, it will take you to the redaction page.

On the redaction, you will come across 3 fields and 2 buttons:
• The first field takes the word/words you want to redact. However, if you are redacting more than one word, you should leave a space between each word.
• The second field takes the character you want in place of the words you want to redact. You should enter just a single character in this field
• The third field is where the body of text you want to redact goes.

After filling all 3 fields, click on the Redact Now button to redact your text. On clicking on this button, you will be redirected to a page with your redacted text.
On this new page, you will come across 1 field, 3 buttons, and a summary of the redaction process:
• The field shows your redacted text.
• The first button is a copy button - for copying the redacted text.
• The second button deletes the content of the field.
• The third button takes you back to the redaction page.
